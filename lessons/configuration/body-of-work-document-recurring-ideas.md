# Build a "Body of Work" Document With Your Core Thesis and Foundational Opinions

## TL;DR

Extract the recurring ideas and stances you repeat across posts and conversations into one markdown file (thesis + 5–8 core opinions); reference it in all content generation to anchor output to your POV.

## Why it matters

Without this, Claude generates generic takes on your topic. With it, Claude knows your lens and adapts ideas to match your philosophy, making all output coherent and opinion-driven instead of consensus mush. Pairs with `brand-voice-profile-reduces-ai-detection` (voice = how you sound; body of work = what you believe).

## How to apply

Gather your past content (LinkedIn posts, podcast transcripts, video scripts, emails from the last 30–90 days). Ask Claude to extract recurring themes and opinions. The output: a one-sentence thesis plus 5–8 core opinions with one-liner explanations (e.g., "Skills are individual units, not monolithic platforms"). Update the file as your thinking evolves; reference it whenever generating marketing, educational, or opinion content.
